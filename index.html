About my Chinese name 王 (Wang) 也 (Ye)

The Chinese surname 王 (wang), meaning "king", is the second most common surname in China. Based on the sixth national census of China, 7.41% of the total population (approximately 88,900,000 people!) hold the same surname as I do.

The Chinese character 也 (yay) might be the mostly commonly used character in ancient Chinese language. However, it has no specific meaning, but is used to finish a sentence, expressing a strong tone of determination.
